Uniform Standards

Cadet Working Dress (CWD)

Jacket

  • Squadron patch on right shoulder/sleeve, located on the velcro
  • Sharp crease going down the sleeves, centred with the New Zealand Flag and Squadron Patch
  • No other creases at all in the jacket
  • Collar ironed down flat on the chest, crease visible down the centre of the collar
  • Chest pockets zipped up
  • No lint or fluff on the uniform
  • Sleeves tight around wrist, otherwise tightly rolled up around the arm
  • Name patch on right side of the chest
  • Rank patch on the centre patch of the chest, right way up

Shirt

  • Unit Shirt or Cadet Forces shirt, tucked into pants
  • Otherwise, black or navy with no logos

Pants

  • No creases
  • No lint or fluff
  • Tightly strapped around the boots

Boots

  • Laces not visible
  • Boots polished all the way around
  • Not falling apart
  • Socks black

No. 3B

Shirt

  • Sharp crease going down the sleeves, centred with the rank slides
  • No other creases at all
  • Collar ironed, flush with the bottom/start of the collar
  • Chest pockets empty
  • Name badge positioned on the right side of the chest, above the chest pocket
  • No lint or fluff on the uniform
  • Buttoned up all the way besides collar button
  • Tucked into the pants
  • Bassard on the right shoulder
  • Rank slides on the shoulders, right way up

Pants

  • Sharp creases going down the front and back
  • No lint or fluff
  • Nothing making the pant pockets bulge
  • Belt's metal part 2 finger spaces out to the left (your POV), feeding through the buckle

Dress Shoes

  • Laces tucked in
  • Polished all the way around
  • Not falling apart
  • Socks black

Jersey

  • Sharp creases down the centre of the arms
  • Rank slides on the shoulders, right way up
  • Jersey buttons on top of the rank slides, crown right way up
  • Spine tucked in under jersey
